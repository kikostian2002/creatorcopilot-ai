# CreatorCopilot AI

CreatorCopilot AI is an AI assistant designed to help short-form content creators generate fresh ideas, avoid repeating previous content, and organize future videos for platforms such as TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

## Problem

Content creators often repeat ideas without realizing it, making their content less engaging and reducing audience retention.

## Solution

CreatorCopilot AI analyzes previously published content and suggests:

- New video ideas
- Similarity detection
- Content planning
- Topic recommendations
- Trend inspiration

## Target Users

- TikTok creators
- Instagram creators
- YouTube Shorts creators

## Technologies

- IBM Bob
- Artificial Intelligence
- GitHub
